About

Butter and Cream is located on the square in downtown Decatur and just off the BeltLine in the Old Fourth Ward. Locally crafted in small batches from real cream and only the freshest ingredients, we make ice cream the way it should be made. While our offerings may have unique, modern twists, they are still reminiscent of classic flavors we have grown up with. All the ingredients infused into our ice cream such as brownies, cookies or toffee have been made fresh on site as well. Support Atlanta’s growing list of locally owned and operated businesses. Get Real. Enjoy the best.
